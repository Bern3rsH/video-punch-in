# Privacy Policy for Video Punch In

**Last updated: 2026-01-16**

## Overview

Video Punch In is a browser extension that helps you copy video URLs with timestamps. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**Video Punch In does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The extension operates entirely locally on your device. No data is sent to any external servers.

## Permissions Explained

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `activeTab` | Access the current tab to detect video elements on the page |
| `scripting` | Inject scripts to read video playback time and display notifications |
| `clipboardWrite` | Copy the generated timestamp URL to your clipboard |
| `contextMenus` | Add a right-click menu option for quick access |

## Data Storage

This extension does not store any data locally or remotely. Each operation is performed in real-time without saving any information.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/yourusername/video-punch-in).

# 隐私政策 - 视频打孔机

**最后更新：2026-01-16**

## 概述

视频打孔机是一款帮助您复制带时间戳视频链接的浏览器扩展。我们致力于保护您的隐私。

## 数据收集

**视频打孔机不收集、存储或传输任何个人数据。**

本扩展完全在您的设备上本地运行，不会向任何外部服务器发送数据。

## 权限说明

本扩展需要以下权限才能正常运行：

| 权限 | 用途 |
|------|------|
| `activeTab` | 访问当前标签页以检测页面上的视频元素 |
| `scripting` | 注入脚本以读取视频播放时间并显示通知 |
| `clipboardWrite` | 将生成的时间戳链接复制到剪贴板 |
| `contextMenus` | 添加右键菜单选项以便快速访问 |

## 数据存储

本扩展不会在本地或远程存储任何数据。每次操作都是实时执行的，不保存任何信息。

## 第三方服务

本扩展不使用任何第三方分析、跟踪或数据收集服务。

## 政策变更

我们可能会不时更新此隐私政策。任何更改都将反映在本文档顶部的"最后更新"日期中。

## 联系方式

如果您对此隐私政策有任何疑问，请在我们的 [GitHub 仓库](https://github.com/yourusername/video-punch-in) 上提出问题。
